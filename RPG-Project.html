The RPG Project was a solo exercise that I took to heart just with having a very strong background with this type of gaming my entire life. I wanted to take the first steps into learning and understanding the big picture of what it would take to develop an RPG; because one day, I would love to release my own multi-platform MMORPG with the inspiration coming from all the games I've played since a young age.

  • Learn initial steps of making an RPG
  • Broaden scope of how to tie together more languages
  • Build a working software program from scratch solo
  • Construct a working simulation of a game
  • Attempt to create a "save"
