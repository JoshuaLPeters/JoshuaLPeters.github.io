Koopatroopa787, that was the username I created on Twitch over five years ago. I didn't realize it at the time but this was the first step in one of the wildest adventures I'd ever experience in my life. It started out as just a hobby to connect with others that shared similar interests. I had a full-time job and was also scheduled to deploy with the Air Force to Kuwait so I had no intention of making Twitch TV a money machine; although, two weeks later after starting I became an official Twitch Partner with permanent front page advertising which also lead to me establishing my first small business!

Super Bowl Commercial

After the first year of broadcasting, Supercell, the Finnish developer and creator of "Clash of Clans"; wanted to include one of my achievements from within their game in their 2015 Winter marketing campaign! It was a commercial that aired globally during the Holidays featuring actor Christoph Waltz; it was also played during the Superbowl. The commercial can be viewed here!

Esports Shoutcasting

In 2017, Summoners War released and started promoting their new competitive feature, Real Time Arena, which involved the top players from around the world. The first major tournament was held at the Amazon M2 Invitational which involved many of the game's personalities and content creators. After finishing 2nd place and a well received tournament production; I was invited to shoutcast alongside professional Poker commentator Ali Nejad and also provided real-time match analytics for Maria Ho, a professional Poker player.

My Community

The friendships that I've made have and always will be the most important thing that I take away from this wild experience. The relationships you forge when there is a common interest such as a game two individuals are passionate about makes creating a close bond very easy. Even when my career takes me into the world of software development and beyond I would never stop broadcasting on Twitch due to all of the amazing people I've gotten to know. If you've watched my channel or supported me in the past and find yourself reading this, I just want to thank you for everything. <3
