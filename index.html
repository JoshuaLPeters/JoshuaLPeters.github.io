About Me

Hello world! My non-traditional path in life has recently taken me through Codeup's web development bootcamp program! I will be graduating at the end of February and will be starting a new career in the tech industry.

For the past five years I've been running my own small business as a Partnered Twitch Broadcaster which started as a hobby when I was about to deploy with the Air Force. This past decade has been a wild ride between the military and the entertainment industry but I am very excited to transition into the world of coding in 2020 as a software developer with all the training Codeup has provided.

Twitch TV Partner

Koopatroopa787, that was the username I created on Twitch over five years ago. I didn't realize it at the time but this was the first step in one of the wildest adventures I'd ever experience in my life. It started out as just a hobby to connect with others that shared similar interests. I had a full-time job and was also scheduled to deploy with the Air Force to Kuwait so I had no intention of making Twitch TV a money machine; although, two weeks later after starting I became an official Twitch Partner with permanent front page advertising which also lead to me establishing my first small business!

Super Bowl Commercial

After the first year of broadcasting, Supercell, the Finnish developer and creator of "Clash of Clans"; wanted to include one of my achievements from within their game in their 2015 Winter marketing campaign! It was a commercial that aired globally during the Holidays featuring actor Christoph Waltz; it was also played during the Superbowl. The commercial can be viewed here!

Air Force Veteran

My military experience includes over six years of service in both active duty and the reserves. I am fully trained as a five level in both Precision Measurement Equipment Laboratory (PMEL) and Aerospace Ground Equipment (AGE). In 2014, I extended my contract so that I could deploy with my unit to Kuwait to support mission Iraqi Freedom. After my return I decided to separate honorably to build my small business as a partnered broadcaster on Twitch and use my GI Bill benefits to continue education. Some of my achievements include:

  • Basic training Honor Graduate.
  • 100% on every PT test during enlistment.
  • Never failed a QA inspection.
  • Deployed to Kuwait and supported Iraqi Freedom 2014.
  • Fully trained in two USAF maintenance professions.

It was an honor to serve in the military; there are days I miss the comradery that it includes but my future is in the tech industry.
