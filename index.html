About Me

Hello world! My non-traditional path in life has recently taken me through Codeup's web development bootcamp program! I will be graduating at the end of February and will be starting a new career in the tech industry.

For the past five years I've been running my own small business as a Partnered Twitch Broadcaster which started as a hobby when I was about to deploy with the Air Force. This past decade has been a wild ride between the military and the entertainment industry but I am very excited to transition into the world of coding in 2020 as a software developer with all the training Codeup has provided.

Software Development

My coding adventure began mid-August starting with the pre-work required to attend Codeup's web development course! The past several months have been crammed with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Java, and projects with my peers from the Deimos Cohort. The instructors enabled each of us to accelerate our learning by providing a clear and concise cirriculum on what the industry wants today from software developers applying for jobs. Here are some of the projects recently completed at Codeup! The Blog is currently under development and our Capstones begin later in February.

Twitch Partner

Koopatroopa787, that was the username I created on Twitch over five years ago. Air Force Veteran

My military experience includes over six years of service in both active duty and the reserves. I am fully trained as a five level in both Precision Measurement Equipment Laboratory (PMEL) and Aerospace Ground Equipment (AGE). In 2014, I extended my contract so that I could deploy with my unit to Kuwait to support mission Iraqi Freedom. After my return I decided to separate honorably to build my small business as a partnered broadcaster on Twitch and use my GI Bill benefits to continue expanding my knowledge.
